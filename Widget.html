Find Permit History & Feature Drawings (E-vault Documents) within an area drawn on map.

Find documents within an area that includes any part of a parcel or street. Please limit query area to 200 acres or 1,000 address points.


Steps to draw selection area:
  • Click starting point on map.
  • Click to continue adding boundary points.
  • Double-click last boundary point to complete.
  • Results will appear in the Permit History and Feature Drawings sections below. Blue text with underlines are links to E-Vault Documents. Blue text without underlines may be clicked to show data location. A CSV file is also created for downloading results and links.
